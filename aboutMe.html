History

Glad you made this far. I would love to tell you more about how I got here

It all started from skiing

Since a kid I've been skiing and loving it. At the age of 15, I wanted to pursue my athletic dream and moved away from home. While completing my high school diploma I was racing around Europe. Eventually, all this led to me getting an offer to compete at NCAA division I skiing in Utah at Westminster College. I said yes to this new adventure which has so far been the best decision in my life. After starting my bachelor's degree in Business I quickly realized my interest towards Computer Science and switched it to be my major. Since then I've been eager to learn more and I noticed this to be my new passion.

Experiences

Last summer I got and opportunity to be part of a software development company in Helsinki, Finland. During my internship I was building frontend side to websites with a help of professionals by using Vue framework. I was lucky enough to received feedback on code optimizing and general learning process from the firm senior engineer. This experince made me realize that software engineering is something I want to do more, and I'm excited to see what the future brings!
